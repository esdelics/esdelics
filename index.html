Tournament Rules & Performance Chart

Tournament Rules

  1. Registration: Submit all required tournament information.
  2. Prize Redemption: Only the In-Game Leader (IGL) can redeem prizes for the team.
  3. Fair Play: No hacking, third-party tools, or teaming up with other squads.
  4. Respect: No abusive or offensive language. Discrimination and harassment are strictly prohibited.
  5. Punctuality: Be on time. Matches start at the scheduled time.
  6. Gameplay: No intentional team killing or sabotage.
  7. Privacy: Respect other players' privacy and rights.
  8. Connection: Players are responsible for their own network connection.
  9. Slot Security: Join the game room in advance to secure your slot.
  10. Recording: Matches will be recorded and uploaded to YouTube.
  11. Refunds: No refund if you do not participate in the match.
  12. Prize during Tie: When there is tie, then WWCD will get the prize.
  13. Reporting: To report a hacker, submit a convincing video of the incident.
  14. Features: All in-game BGMI features (Self AED, Emergency Pickup, etc.) are allowed.
  15. Timings: The ROOM ID will be given 15 minutes before the match timing.
