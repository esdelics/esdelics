Tournament Rules & Scoring System

Tournament Rules

By registering, you automatically agree to all rules.

General & Administrative Rules
  1. Must be 16+ years old (minors need parent consent).
  2. Must be Level 25 and Platinum I tier or above.
  3. Indian citizenship with valid ID proof required(if needed).
  4. Team structure: Can play as Solo, Duo, Trio or Squad.
  5. Registration: Submit all required tournament information.
  6. Punctuality: Be on time. Matches start at the scheduled time.
  7. Refunds: No refund if you do not participate in the match.
Code of Conduct
  1. Zero tolerance for cheating, hacking, or exploiting.
  2. No match-fixing, collusion, or account sharing.
  3. Respect all players, organizers, and officials.
  4. Follow all instructions from tournament officials.
  5. Fair Play: No hacking, third-party tools, or teaming up with other squads.
  6. Respect: No abusive or offensive language. Discrimination and harassment are strictly prohibited.
  7. Gameplay: No intentional team killing or sabotage.
Technical Rules
  1. Only mobile phones allowed (no emulators/controllers).
  2. Reporting an Issue: POV recording (in-game or handcam).
  3. Loot settings: Loot will be random.
  4. Connection: Players are responsible for their own network connection.
  5. Banned: All in-game BGMI features [Self AED, Emergency Pickup, Flare Gun, Telepads (in Sanhok) & Streaming] are NOT ALLOWED.
Operational Rules
  1. Slot Security: Join the game room in advance to secure your slot.
  2. Recording: Matches will be recorded and uploaded to YouTube.
  3. Reporting: To report a hacker, submit a convincing video of the incident.
  4. Timings: The ROOM ID will be given 15 minutes before the match timing.
Prize Rules
  1. Prize Redemption: Only the In-Game Leader (IGL) can redeem prizes for the team.
  2. Prize during Tie: When there is a tie, then WWCD (Winner Winner Chicken Dinner) will get the prize.
Organizers reserve the right to make changes to rules at any time.

Violation to any rules can disqualify the whole team.
